EM sizes

Easy resizing

The whole template uses EM sizes: it allows to resize any element just by changing its font-size property. To do so, you have some classes with custom font-sizes:

.bigger
Font-size: 2.5em
.big
Font-size: 1.5em
.small
Font-size: 0.833em
.smaller
Font-size: 0.75em

To resize an element, just add any of these classes to it:

			<button class="small">Button</button>

			<button>Button</button>

			<button class="big">Button</button>

			<button class="bigger">Button</button>

Most effects will scale too, but note that some styles using images (icons mainly) may not scale properly.
